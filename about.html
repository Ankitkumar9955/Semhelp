Why SemHelp?

In a huge university like SRM University, with thousands of students and hundreds of exams every semester, it can be overwhelming to find the right study materials, notes, and previous year questions (PYQs) when you need them most.

SemHelp was created to solve this problem. We bring together all essential resources in one place, making it easy for students to access, share, and support each other—especially during the stressful exam season. No more endless searching, no more last-minute panic. Just organized, reliable help for every subject and every unit.

Our Mission

To empower SRM students by providing a centralized, modern, and supportive platform for all semester study needs. Whether you're looking for notes, PYQs, or peer support, SemHelp is your academic companion.
